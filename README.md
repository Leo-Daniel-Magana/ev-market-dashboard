# 🌍 Global EV Market Dashboard | IEA 2024

## Overview
This dashboard analyzes global electric vehicle adoption trends 
using the International Energy Agency (IEA) 2024 dataset, covering 
2010–2035 across 54 regions.

## 🔍 Key Insights
- **Global EV share surged** from 0.08% in 2010 to 26.41% in 2023 — a 330x increase driven largely by post-2019 policy momentum
- **China dominates** with 102.80% cumulative EV sales share vs Europe at 43.80% and USA at just 10.27%
- **BEV is winning** — Battery Electric Vehicles are outselling Plug-in Hybrids 2:1 and accelerating rapidly post-2020
- **Future outlook** — Under optimistic projections, global EV sales could exceed 150M vehicles annually by 2035
